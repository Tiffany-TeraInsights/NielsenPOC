2Q2R App

The 2Q2R app allows you to use your phone as the second factor authenticator. The app uses eliptic curve digital signature algorithm based on the NIST P-256 curve to securely authenticate. In essence, the private key generated on the phone (and not extractable), is used to digitally sign challenges generated and latter verified by the server.

To use the 2Q2R app, you need to install it from the Play or Apple store.
