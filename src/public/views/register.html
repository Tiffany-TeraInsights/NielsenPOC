This is a demo for the 2Q2R two factor authenticator app.
Two factor authentication (2FA) is a method of authentication that requires the user to provide multiple pieces of proof that they are who they say they are. 2Q2R specifically uses a QR code in combination with a mobile phone as a seperate authenticator. 2Q2R also supports Universal 2nd Factor (U2F) devices.
